Approach

“I turn real moments into cinematic memory.”

The same sensibility runs through the film work and the still images: character first, atmosphere always, and form in service of feeling. Whether the medium is animation, hybrid storytelling, or photography, the aim is the same: make the image stay alive after the viewer leaves.

About

Reinis Kalnaellis is a Latvian director, photographer, and visual storyteller based in Riga. His public body of work is strongest in animation, while his broader practice also extends into photographic series, hybrid visual development, and author-led world-building.

Professional identity

Film-first, image-led, and multidisciplinary without becoming diffuse. This front page is designed to make that immediately legible: an experienced director with festival credibility, an emerging photographic voice with a strong point of view, and a collaborator who can shape projects from concept to emotionally resonant image.

What I bringDirection, visual storytelling, atmosphere, character sense, concept development, and the ability to connect handcrafted emotion with contemporary tools.
What I care aboutImages that feel lived in, memorable, and honest. Work that can move between children’s worlds, poetic documentary sensibility, and more mature mythic or surreal territory.

Best fit collaborations

Producers seeking a director with a distinct visual voice. Artists who want cinematic portraiture or campaign imagery. Cultural spaces, festivals, theatres, and cross-media projects that benefit from a filmmaker’s approach to tone and narrative.

Ideal clientsProduction companies, artists, labels, festivals, institutions, boutique brands, and creative teams that want a world, not just content.
Working styleThoughtful, visually exacting, fast in idea generation, and comfortable moving between high-level vision and practical execution.
